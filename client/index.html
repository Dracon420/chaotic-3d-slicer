Adhesion & support

          
          
          

            
            
          

          
Tree supports use less material and peel off easier; Normal is sturdier under flat overhangs. Z gap is the air space between support and model — larger = easier removal, smaller = better surface.

          
          

            
            
          

          
A skirt primes the nozzle with a loop around the print. More lines = better priming; more height (layers) makes it a taller wall that peels off in one piece.
